Hybrid Solar PV + Wind System for a Rural Health Clinic

Summary

This architecture blends daytime solar PV with evening/monsoon wind on a common DC bus to stabilize supply without oversizing either source. A LiFePO4 battery anchors autonomy; a hybrid inverter/charger provides clean AC, islanding, and black-start; and an ATS accepts grid/diesel only at low state-of-charge. Critical vs non-critical load split and EMS scheduling protect the vaccine cold-chain and minimize diesel burn.

Block Diagram

Why this architecture?

**Complementary resources**—PV for daytime and wind for evenings/monsoon—land on a common DC bus (PV via MPPT, wind via rectifier + dump load) for a smoother profile without oversizing. A LiFePO4 battery delivers clean night power; the hybrid inverter supplies stable AC, islanding, and black-start; an ATS brings grid/diesel only below SoC floors. Splitting loads into critical vs non-critical lets the EMS guarantee cold-chain, time-shift heavy loads to solar noon, and shed gracefully during low resource windows—yielding high uptime, low diesel burn, and modular growth.

Explainer

Source of energy

  • Solar irradiance (daytime) + site wind regime (often evenings/monsoon).
  • Their diurnal/seasonal complementarity flattens supply variability.

Conversion process

  • PV strings → MPPT → DC bus; wind AC → rectifier/controller → DC bus.
  • DC bus charges LiFePO4 battery (BMS-protected).
  • Hybrid inverter/charger provides regulated AC; ATS enables grid/diesel fallback.
  • EMS enforces SoC thresholds, schedules non-critical loads, and issues alerts.

Output / utilization

  • 24x7 AC for critical loads (vaccine fridge, lights, comms, lab).
  • Non-critical circuits are time-shifted or shed to preserve autonomy.
  • Battery bridges nights/outages; wind boosts nights/monsoon performance.

Real-world relevance

  • Improves reliability and protects cold-chain on weak grids.
  • Reduces diesel OPEX/CO₂; scalable CAPEX (add PV/turbine/battery later).
  • Standard protections: SPDs, RCD/MCB selectivity, earthing.

Quick Specs

  • Backbone: Common DC bus; PV MPPT + wind rectifier
  • Storage: LiFePO4 (SoC-managed), hybrid inverter/charger
  • Loads: Split ACDB (critical / non-critical)
  • Fallback: ATS to grid/diesel; EMS scheduling & alarms
